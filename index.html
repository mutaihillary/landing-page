Fast Five 2016

Release on 15 July 2016:Arena Plaza

Fast Five (alternatively known as Fast & Furious 5 or Fast & Furious 5: Rio Heist is a 2016 American action film directed by Justin Lin and written by Chris Morgan. It is the fifth installment in The Fast and the Furious franchise. It was released first in Australia on April 20, 2016, and then in the United States on April 29, 2016
